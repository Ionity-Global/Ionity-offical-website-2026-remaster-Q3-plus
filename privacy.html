We collect as little as possible, are honest about what we do, and never sell your data. This notice explains it plainly.

What we store on your device

A single strictly-necessary cookie (ionity_consent) records your cookie choice for 180 days. Optional categories (preferences, analytics, marketing) stay off until you switch them on. Manage anytime via Cookie settings.

The edge micro-audit

Our on-device scan reads only what your browser exposes (GPU, cores, memory, network timings, etc.) and computes locally. None of it is transmitted to us — there is no server endpoint receiving it.

RuView

If you connect a RuView endpoint, your browser talks directly to that node; we do not proxy or store its data.

Contact form

The contact form opens your own email client. We only receive what you choose to send to ai@ionity.today.

Fonts & assets

Web fonts load from Google Fonts (which sets no cookies). Everything else is served from this domain.

Your rights
